The blood donation camp organized by the Panadura Municipal Council in association with the Migettuwatte Gunananda Thero Memorial Public Library and the Lions Club will be held on the 12th of this month. 8.30 am to 1.30 pm at Panadura Town Hall.

With the participation of the Hon. Mayor, the Hon. Deputy Mayor, the Hon. Municipal Councilors and the Hon. Secretary on 22nd January 2020 at Pirith Pinkama was held at 8.30 am at Udahamulla Branch Library
